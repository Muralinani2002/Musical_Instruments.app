Types of Instruments

Stringed Musical Instrument

Stringed Musical instruments produces Music by a stretched strings EX:Guitar,Sitar.

Wind Musical Instruments

These instruments produce music by vibration of air columns inside them EX:Flute,Shehnai.

Membrane Musical Instruments

These instruments produces music by vibration of thin stretched membrane. EX:Tabla,Drums.

Plate type Musical Instruments

These instruments produces music by vibration of thick plates. EX:Cymbals,Jal-tarang.
